About Engineering Business Group

EBG is a student-run organization dedicated to helping engineers explore business-facing careers such as technical sales, product management, business development, and consulting.

We host speaker events, resume workshops, and skill-building sessions to prepare members for these roles. In the future, we aim to launch collaborative projects with real businesses and alumni partners.
